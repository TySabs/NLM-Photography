Natasha L Maczko - Professional Photography with Style

My name is Natasha Maczko. I have been doing professional photography for over 12 years.

Between working for a studio and freelancing, I love to share my work. Past, present and future. My style is ever changing so feel free to check back time to time and for any specials or promotions

I mostly specialize in Weddings, Engagement, and Portrait sessions. But I also have experience with New Borns, Families, Seniors, Fashion Models and Bands. I also specialize in invite, thank you and Wedding Album design.

I am interested in everything and anything photography, to creating memories to cherish for a life time or to creating art to hang on your walls and admire, I feel blessed to have found my passion in life. I am a very open artist and I am always looking to grow and prosper as one. Capturing any event or moment is a true art form in itself.
